Code-Compliant Upgrades

Ageing electrical infrastructure is a liability. We assess, design, and execute upgrades that bring your facility into compliance, improve capacity, and reduce the risk of costly failures.

Every upgrade project includes a detailed scope document, permit applications on all required work, and a final inspection — so you receive documented proof of code compliance at project close.

  • Service entrance upgrades (200A–2000A)
  • Distribution board replacement
  • Branch circuit rewiring
  • Grounding and bonding upgrades
  • Arc flash hazard mitigation
  • Load analysis and capacity planning
