Control Panel Design & Build

Custom-engineered industrial control panels — from concept and schematic design through fabrication, installation, and full commissioning.

Service Overview

What We Deliver

Outdated or inefficient systems lead to costly downtime and safety risks. Our solutions are engineered to improve reliability, increase efficiency, and ensure your systems operate at peak performance for the long term.

The Pinewood Approach

We begin every project with a thorough on-site assessment to understand your operational requirements, existing infrastructure, and performance goals. From there, we design a custom system architecture that integrates seamlessly with your facility.

All work is performed by licensed professionals and documented completely — including as-built drawings, test reports, and system manuals — so your team has everything needed for long-term operations.

Step 0101

Design & Engineering

Detailed schematic design, panel layout, and component specification to meet your application requirements.

Step 0202

Fabrication

Panel fabrication using quality components with full wire labelling and professional termination.

Factory Testing
