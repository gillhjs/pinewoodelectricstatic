Control Panels

Control Panel Design & Build

Custom-engineered industrial control panels fabricated to UL/CSA standards — designed for scalability, safety, and decades of reliable operation.

Engineering-Led Panel Builds

We design, fabricate, wire, and test industrial control panels for every application — from motor control centers and power distribution panels to custom automation enclosures and relay logic panels.

Our panels are built with premium components, complete wire labelling, and full documentation — so your maintenance team can work on them with confidence long after installation.

  • Detailed schematic design (AutoCAD)
  • Panel layout and component selection
  • Professional fabrication and wiring
  • Factory acceptance testing (FAT)
  • Site installation and field wiring
  • Full documentation package
01

Design & Engineering
