PLC Programming & Industrial Automation

Custom PLC and HMI programming, SCADA integration, and full automation system design — engineered to maximize production efficiency and minimize costly downtime.

What We Deliver

We design and implement PLC-based automation systems for manufacturing, processing, and industrial facilities across the Lower Mainland. From single-machine control to plant-wide SCADA, our systems are engineered to your exact operational requirements.

Every project begins with a thorough process assessment. We document the existing system, define the control philosophy, and design a solution that integrates cleanly with your existing infrastructure — minimizing changeover time and risk.

  • Siemens, Allen-Bradley, Mitsubishi & more
  • HMI design and development
  • SCADA integration and historian
  • Remote monitoring capability
  • Full technical documentation handover
  • Operator training included
