EV Charger Installation

Level 2 EV charger installation for homes and commercial properties — fully permitted, inspected to BC code, and eligible for BC Hydro and CleanBC rebates.

Fast, Clean EV Charger Installs

We handle the full EV charger installation process — from electrical panel assessment and dedicated circuit wiring to charger mounting, permit application, and final inspection.

We work with all major residential and commercial charger brands including Tesla Wall Connector, ChargePoint, JuiceBox, FLO, and more. All installations are eligible for applicable BC Hydro and CleanBC rebates.

  • Level 2 (240V) home & commercial chargers
  • All major brands supported
  • Dedicated circuit installation
  • Permit pulled and inspection scheduled
  • BC Hydro / CleanBC rebate eligible
  • Indoor and outdoor installations
01

Site Assessment

Evaluate panel capacity, circuit routing, and optimal charger placement.

02

Permit Application

We pull all required electrical permits before work begins.

03

Installation

Dedicated circuit wiring, panel connection, and charger mounting.

04

Inspection & Rebate

Final inspection coordination and rebate documentation assistance.

Included in This Service

  • Tesla Wall Connector
  • ChargePoint Home Flex
  • JuiceBox 40/48
  • FLO Home X5
  • Siemens VersiCharge
  • Any NEMA 14-50 outlet
  • Commercial multi-port
  • Load management systems
